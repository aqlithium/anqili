Anqi Li

About Me

Hello! I recently graduated from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology with a B.Sc. in Mathematics (Course 18). I am broadly interested in combinatorics and theoretical computer science. Particularly, I am interested in extremal and probabilistic combinatorics, as well as the applications of discrete Fourier analytic and probabilistic methods in additive combinatorics and theoretical computer science.

For the upcoming academic year, I will be pursuing a Master of Advanced Study (Part III of the Mathematical Tripos) at University of Cambridge, Trinity College supported by a Trinity Studentship in Mathematics. Thereafter, I will start my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Stanford University as a Stanford EDGE Doctoral Fellow.
