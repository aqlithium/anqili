Anqi Li

About Me

I am a fourth year undergraduate at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, majoring in mathematics (Course 18). I am broadly interested in combinatorics and theoretical computer science. Particularly, I am interested in extremal and probabilistic combinatorics, as well as the applications of discrete Fourier analytic and polynomial methods in additive combinatorics and theoretical computer science.
